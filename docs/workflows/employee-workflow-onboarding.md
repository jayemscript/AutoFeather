# HRM (Human Resource Management) Workflow

## **Optional: Recruitment & Hiring (Before "New Employee")**

*This entire section is optional if the employee is already hired.*

1. **Job Posting**
   Company posts vacancy.

2. **Applicant Screening**
   Review resumes and shortlist candidates.

3. **Interviews**
   Conduct interviews and assessments.

4. **Hiring Decision**
   Finalize candidate selection and prepare job offer.

---

## **Main HRM Onboarding Workflow**

```
New employee created
    ↓
Employee record creation
    ↓
HR validation (completeness check)
    ↓
Document verification (authenticity check)
    ↓
Status: Verified
    ↓
Attach job information (position, department, schedule, salary)
    ↓
Approval workflow (HR → Department Head → Payroll)
    ↓
User account creation (HRIS + biometric/attendance ID)
    ↓
Employee activation
    ↓
Employee can now clock in/out
```
